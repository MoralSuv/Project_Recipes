Skillet Chicken Thighs

Back Home

Description

Skillet chicken thighs with really saucy sauce. It is finger licking good!

Ingredients:

  • boneless chicken thighs
  • salt and pepper
  • olive oil
  • minced garlic
  • dried thyme
  • rosemary (fresh if available)
  • chicken broth
  • sun-dried tomatoes
  • heavy cream
  • grated parmesan
